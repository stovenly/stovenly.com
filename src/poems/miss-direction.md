title: Miss Direction

drizzle heavy eyelids
on sleepy tea, and wander
into bed with me
keep me cozy, comfy,
company

i'm boiling with dreams, feelings
of sheets between you
and what i see, komorebi light
through screens of burning leaves

torrents of fire
sear me alive, moments of desire
of pools and your eyes,
of drowning and dives
to die in your arms, with lips on your thighs

like hope in strange and ancient love
ilke open trains on walls above
gusting through on heaven's gun
your presence leaves my life undone

so wrap me up, and package me
keep me safe, i'm fragile, please
this side up, lift with the knees
put me down, and carefully

put me where you wanted me
put me where I want to be
inside your heart, the coeur de lys
a petal each for each of me

and scents of love and laughing leaves
of mists and morning memories
of life with you, my lovely lys
my coeur d'abri, my waine
