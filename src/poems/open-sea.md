title: Open Sea

I sat by the ocean
listening to the terns.
The waves broke to the same song
static hiss, static hiss, storm call.
The water sank into the sand
left it darker, like losing a person
leaves you darker.

With each ebb the ground rose and sank
briefly exposing the naked shells
of little white pebbles
gray ones,
black ones,
static hiss.
