title: Footprints In Ash
layout: poem.njk

a great dragon wing
another end of the world
to fire and flame

our brick turned to slag
the king's castle is burning
guess it's time to leave

the wagon's wheel caught
on some poor molten child
now he's in pieces

stifling heat took us
what a time to sell linen
what a place to die
