Meteor Mail (1)

emergency transmission received from: outpost[bingo]
user blocking preferences overridden by emergency protocol
begin sequencing data stream

"hello, lily,
stars,
moon,
heavens.

there is no gravity in space
(infinitesimals don't count to me)
but an attraction takes place
from you to me (not gravity)

no, all of space could
not eclipse what i feel.
when i saw you in that yellow maintenance tunic
my airlock shattered (not really, please
don't send a maintenance drone again
it cost me a fortune)

lily, my lover,
come dance with me
from one orbit from another
on rings of debris, on rings
of glee, and once you see
my starlight heart
your wish will be
to marry me"

emergency transmission end
